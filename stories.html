January 03, 2026

100+ Trees planted across local communities

Hope Nest Community NGO joined local leaders, schools and community volunteers in a successful tree-planting campaign aimed at restoring the environment and promoting climate awareness. More than 100 indigenous trees were planted in schools, churches and public spaces.

Beyond planting trees, participants learned about proper tree care, environmental conservation and the importance of protecting natural resources for future generations. The campaign strengthened community participation while creating a greener and healthier environment.

June 01, 2025

Life skills workshop inspires young girls

Hope Nest Community NGO organized a life skills workshop that brought together girls from different communities to learn about hands-on economic skills, leadership, self-confidence, reproductive health, communication and goal setting.

The interactive sessions encouraged participants to believe in their potential, stay in school and make informed decisions about their futures. Mentors also shared personal experiences that inspired the girls to pursue education and become future leaders within their communities.

March 29, 2024

Community cleanup campaign promotes a healthy environment

Community members, volunteers and youth participated in a large scale environmental cleanup campaign organized by Hope Nest Community NGO. Together they collected waste from public areas, cleared drainage channels and educated residents on proper waste disposal.

The activity demonstrated the power of community action in creating cleaner neighbourhoods while encouraging responsible environmental practices. Hope Nest continues to promote conservation initiatives that improve public health and protect the environment for generations to come.
