Wellness & Protection

A thriving community is built on more than economic opportunity, it is built on the well-being, safety, and dignity of its people. Across our communities, many elderly individuals live in isolation and abandonment, women struggle with the trauma of domestic violence, girls face the devastating consequences of abuse, and vulnerable families lack access to the support they need to heal and rebuild their lives.

HOPE NEST believes that every person deserves to live free from violence, fear, and discrimination. Through our Wellness & Protection Program, we provide life-changing services that promote mental, emotional, and physical well-being while creating safer and more resilient communities. Working alongside donors, volunteers, community leaders, and development partners, we deliver professional counseling, psychosocial support, mental health services, and protection programs that help vulnerable individuals recover, regain confidence, and rebuild their futures. We also provide Sexual and Reproductive Health (SRH) education, strengthen community awareness, and advocate for the rights and protection of women, girls, the elderly, and other at-risk groups.

Help Strengthen the Supports to Age Well

Aging well is not a solo journey. It takes strong systems and support for all of us maintain our health and economic well being now and in the future.

With your support today, we can create the conditions for everyone to age well. This means not just helping one individual but changing programs and policies to ensure every one of us has the basic support we need to enjoy our gift of longevity regardless of who we are or where we live.

Get Involved

Healing and protecting vulnerable communities requires everyone to play a part. Whether you are an individual, organization, professional, or business, your support helps us create safer, healthier, and more resilient communities.

Partnership

  • Supporting community counseling and mental health programs.
  • Sponsoring awareness campaigns on domestic violence and child protection.
  • Providing in-kind donations such as counseling materials, hygiene kits, food, or clothing.
  • Funding safe spaces and community outreach initiatives.
  • Collaborating on advocacy, research, or community development projects
  • Offering professional expertise in health, legal aid, education, or social work.

Volunteer

  • Support community awareness and outreach events.
  • Facilitate life skills, wellness, or empowerment sessions.
  • Mentor women, youth, or survivors rebuilding their lives.
  • Assist with counseling programs (qualified professionals).
  • Help organize fundraising and community events.
  • Support office administration, communications, or social media.
  • Visit and support elderly people living alone.

Donations

Your Donation Can Make a Difference

  • $100 – Provides a dignity kit with essential hygiene supplies for a vulnerable woman or girl.
  • $1000 – Supports emergency food and basic necessities for a family in crisis.
  • $2000 – Helps train a community volunteer to identify and respond to cases of abuse and neglect.
  • $100 – Facilitates one professional counseling and psychosocial support session for a survivor of trauma.
  • $1000 – Supports a community awareness session on domestic violence, child protection, and mental health.
  • $1000 – Funds a Sexual and Reproductive Health (SRH) education workshop for adolescents and young women.
  • $5000 – Sponsors a community outreach program reaching vulnerable families with counseling, education, and protection services.
  • $1,000 – Helps establish or sustain a safe community space where women, girls, children, and older persons can access support and referrals.
  • $2,500 – Enables a comprehensive community wellness and protection campaign, including counseling, awareness activities, volunteer training, and emergency assistance for vulnerable households.
